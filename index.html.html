Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our affiliate program

How do I join the GROWZIO Affiliate Program?

Simply fill out the application form on this page. Our team will review your application and get back to you within 24 hours with your affiliate dashboard access and unique referral link.

How much commission will I earn?

Commission rates range from 20% to 30% depending on your sales volume. New affiliates start at 20%, and you can progress to higher tiers as your sales increase.

How and when will I get paid?

Payouts are processed via JazzCash, Easypaisa, or Bank Transfer. Payout frequency depends on your tier: Weekly for Starter, every 3 days for Pro, and instantly for Elite affiliates.

Is there any cost to join?

No, joining our affiliate program is completely free. There are no hidden fees or minimum requirements to get started.

What marketing materials do you provide?

We provide banners, social media posts, WhatsApp templates, and promotional content that you can use to promote GROWZIO. Higher tiers get access to custom marketing materials.

Can I track my performance?

Yes, you'll get access to a personalized affiliate dashboard where you can track clicks, conversions, commissions, and payout history in real-time.
